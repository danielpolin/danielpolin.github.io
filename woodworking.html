Woodworking

Overview

Woodworking is a hobby of mine. Here are some of my projects.

Redwood and Pine Shelf

I decided I needed a shelf to hold the boardgames and such that I might have in the future. I designed a two-tone (pine and redwood) shelf and set to work. I added a drawer for all those things I might want to store in my living room. I had to make everything with hand tools as I still don't have access to the Davis machine shop. It might have been a little more precise if I had had a power saw, but I think it turned out alright.

Redwood Coffee Table

I recently moved to the west coast and found that the lumber yards carry redwood. Apparently it is perfectly renewable. Where the east coast has cedar, the west has redwood. So I made a coffee table out of it. The grain is beautiful.

Cedar Stool

I made this stool from red cedar. The legs and supports are from a single 4X4 that was lying around from a previous project and the seat is a cut and glued from a 2X4. I then drew and wood burned the rose design. The last photo is post oiling.
